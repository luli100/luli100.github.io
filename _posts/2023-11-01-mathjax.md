### MathJax 多行公式对齐

```
$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
\hat x_{n,n} = w_1 \cdot z_n + (1-w_1) \cdot \hat x_{n,n-1} \\\\ 
&= w_1 \cdot z_n + \hat x_{n,n-1} - w_1 \cdot \hat x_{n,n-1} \\\\
&= \hat x_{n,n-1} + w_1 \cdot (z_n - \hat x_{n,n-1})
\end{split}
\nonumber
\end{equation}
$$
```
